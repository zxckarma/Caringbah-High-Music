Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to own an instrument to join band?

No! The school has a range of instruments available for free. We recommend starting with a school instrument to make sure you enjoy playing before investing in your own. Our music staff can advise on suitable instruments for beginners.

When does the band rehearse?

The training band rehearses every Tuesday after school from 3:15-4:30pm. The concert band meets Thursday mornings before school at 7:30am and Friday afternoons 3:15-4:45pm. Students are expected to attend all rehearsals for their ensemble.

Are there band camps or trips?

Yes! We have an annual band camp in Term 2 where students spend a weekend focused on music and team building, they will go over music played in their respective ensemble whilst also having great amounts of fun with their friends!

Are private lessons required?

While not strictly required, we strongly recommend private lessons to help students progress faster. The school can connect you with our network of excellent peripatetic teachers who offer lessons on campus at competitive rates.
