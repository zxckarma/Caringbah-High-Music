Our Programs

Beginner Band

Perfect for students new to music. Learn the basics of playing an instrument and reading music.

Schedule: Thursdays, 3-4pm

Instructor: Mr. Timothy Sampson

Requirements: No prior experience needed. Instruments provided.

  • Introduction to musical instruments
  • Basic music theory and notation
  • Fundamental playing techniques
  • Group playing experience

Intermediate Band

For students with some experience. Develop your skills and prepare for performances.

Schedule: Mondays, 3-4pm

Instructor: Mr Timothy Sampson

Requirements: 6+ months experience or instructor approval

  • Intermediate music theory
  • Technique refinement
  • Sight-reading practice
  • Ensemble coordination

Advanced Band

For experienced musicians. Focus on advanced techniques and participate in competitions.

Schedule: Monday-Friday, Called During School Hours

Instructor: Mr. Adrian Tuero

Requirements: 1+ years experience

  • Advanced musical interpretation
  • Competition preparation
  • One on One
  • Leadership development
